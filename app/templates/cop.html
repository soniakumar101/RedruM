COP

General Rules: 1.) You need to have at least 8 or more players. There will be two Killers, one Cop, and the rest will be the Prey 2.) The game is divided into two phases: the phase where the Killers are allowed to kill one victim at a time and the phase where the Prey can talk to each other and the Cop. The Cop cannot be killed and everyone will know who the Cop is throughout the game. 3.) During the phase where the Killers can choose a victim they can talk amongst themselves to strategically decide which members to kill off. During the phase where the Prey can guess, everyone can talk to each other to figure out who the Killers are, while the Killers can lie and throw suspicion off of them. 4.)The Cop is the sole character who can enter in guesses for who the Killers are after everyone agrees on who to vote for. 5.) The game continues until either the Killers are discovered or enough of the Prey have been killed off and the Killers are the majority.

The Role of Cop: As the Cop your purpose is to not only aid the Prey during the guessing rounds but to also enter in the guesses for which the majority of the group believe to be the Killers. Try not to let the Killers confuse you or the Prey!
